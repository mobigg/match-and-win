Privacy Policy — Match & Win

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Match & Win ("we", "the app") is a puzzle game published by Mobigg. This policy explains what information the app uses and how.

Information we collect

  • Game progress — level unlocks and star ratings stored locally on your device (AsyncStorage). We do not operate a login or cloud save unless you add one later.
  • Settings — music, sound effects, and haptics preferences stored on your device.
  • Advertising data — Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to show and measure ads. See Google Privacy Policy.

Third-party services

  • Google AdMob — interstitial ads (e.g. after some level wins, optional free hint). AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies. You can limit ad personalization in your device Google settings.
  • Google Play — if you install from Play Store, Google processes purchase and device data per its policies.

Permissions

The app may request audio-related permissions so piano sounds and optional background music can play. We do not record your microphone or access your camera.

Children

The app is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

Data retention

Local game data remains on your device until you clear app storage or uninstall the app. Ad partners retain data according to their own policies.

Your choices

  • Turn off Line piano and Background piano in Settings.
  • Reset ad personalization: Android Settings → Google → Ads.
  • Uninstall the app to remove local data.

Changes
